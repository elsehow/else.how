I live between research and critical technical practice (a la Phil Agre). Lately, I'm interested in structural risks to the global Internet and that internet's discontents---paranets, local networks, and alternative internets. You can follow that work on my substack.

I've worked on a variety of topics through my career. A high-level list, in roughly reverse-chronological order.

  • Machine learning bias and fairness
  • Games for identifying security threats
  • Social aspects of biosensing
  • Speculative and future-oriented methods
  • Brain-based authentication (passthoughts)
  • Cognitive science, particularly spatial memory

I'm sure there are throughlines between these projects, but to be honest, I don't look for them. I do whatever interests me. If you find any patterns, let me know.
